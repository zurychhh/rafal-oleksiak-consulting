## User Journey Map

### Phase 1: Awareness (First Visit)
**Entry Points**: Google search, LinkedIn post, referral
**Goal**: Understand who Rafał is, what he does
**Content**: Hero section, company logos, quick credibility signals
**CTA**: None yet - building trust

### Phase 2: Consideration (Exploring)
**Actions**: Read services, view case studies, check process
**Goal**: Evaluate fit for their needs
**Content**: Detailed service descriptions, ROI examples, methodology
**CTA**: Soft - "Learn more", "See case study"

### Phase 3: Evaluation (Comparing)
**Actions**: Deep-dive into case studies, check LinkedIn, maybe Google Rafał
**Goal**: Compare vs other consultants, validate credibility
**Content**: Full case studies, testimonials, LinkedIn profile, blog posts
**CTA**: Medium - "Download calculator", "Subscribe to newsletter"

### Phase 4: Decision (Ready to Act)
**Actions**: Fill contact form, book consultation, download lead magnet
**Goal**: Initiate conversation
**Content**: Contact form, Calendly, lead magnet landing page
**CTA**: Strong - "Book Free Consultation", "Get Your ROI Report"

### Phase 5: Nurture (Post-Contact)
**Actions**: Email sequence, follow-up, proposal
**Goal**: Convert lead to client
**Content**: Automated emails, case studies, pricing proposal
**CTA**: "Sign contract", "Schedule kick-off"
